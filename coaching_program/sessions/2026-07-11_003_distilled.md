# Distilled — 2026-07-11_003

**Topic:** The `🫡` reply to the client's daily GM. Resolved and applied to the
plan (rev `003`).

## Decision (now in Layer 1 "daily loop")
- **Bare GM → reply `🫡`.** One non-escalating token: *received, carry on*.
  Never praise, tally, or reference the streak.
- **A GM carrying anything more than the bare greeting → do NOT fire `🫡`.** A
  reflexive salute to a message that said something real reads as the coach not
  listening. Such a message falls outside the ritual and is "not answered by the
  default reply."
- Timeline still never disclosed (unchanged).

## Why `🫡` is the right default (rationale, not all written into plan)
Non-escalating (doesn't reintroduce the countdown/grading the method kills),
symmetric (one token in, one token back), and sustainable for the coach at scale.

## Deliberate scope boundaries
- **No clients yet** — designing the rule in the abstract; optimize for what the
  method demands, not current load.
- Handling of content-carrying GMs is **intentionally left undesigned.** Building
  "match on content" logic now would be the same front-loading trap Layer 1's
  onboarding warns against.
- Wording rec applied: the plan does **not** say the literal words "no response"
  (a future coach could misread that as "ignore the client" — the same
  not-listening failure). It says the default reply doesn't fire.
- The "we'll refine responses case-by-case in the future" note is **context
  between user and coach — deliberately NOT written into the plan.**

## Open question / next step
- If/when real clients exist: verify the client actually reads `🫡` as
  salute/respect (emoji semantics) — filed as a Day-Zero check, not addressed

- Case-by-case handling of content-carrying GMs is the future work, undefined.
