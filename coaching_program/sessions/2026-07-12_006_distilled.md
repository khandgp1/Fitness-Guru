## The proposed food ladder (candidate, NOT yet in the plan)
Every rung is a number-free behavior, weekly binary, stacks on all prior habits. The deficit is **emergent, not a dedicated brick** — engineered as a side effect of "adding good food," never announced.

- **B1** *(exists in plan)* — plain 0% Greek yogurt, empty the tub.
- **B2** — one meal/day built around a palm of lean protein.
- **B3** — extend the palm to every main meal (1 palm ♀ / 2 ♂).
- **B4** — add a fist or two of produce to each main meal.
- **B5** — eat protein + produce *first*, before starch/fat. **← deficit ignites here (B4→B5).**
- **B6** — swap caloric drinks for water/unsweetened.
- **B7** — replace most-frequent snack with a protein/produce default.
- **B8** — one plate of starch/fat, no seconds (the only restriction rung; last on purpose).
- **B9** *(optional)* — cap/standardize alcohol (kept separate from B6).

Deficit mechanism: ignition B4→B5 (volume + eat-it-first shrinks the dense remainder), deepening B6/B7 (kills liquid sugar + grazing), consolidation B8. Protein (B2/B3) is the *enabling condition* — makes the emergent deficit burn fat not muscle — not itself a calorie cut. Protein expressed as palms, never grams (coach's hidden target ~1.2–1.6 g/kg for high-BF clients).
